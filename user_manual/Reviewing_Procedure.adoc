== Reviewer Workflow

Reviewers are the *final quality control layer*, ensuring integrity of public-facing
YouTube content. Reviewers may overwrite any timestamp or thumbnail chosen by an
Editor if they lack precision.

[CAUTION]
====
*Coordinate with other volunteers before reviewing.*
====

=== Step by Step

. *Select the room* from the Status List containing the talk to review.
. *Select the talk* you want to review.
. *Use the YouTube player* to confirm the start and end times selected by the Editor.
. *Confirm the metadata* — ensure the video content matches the title and abstract. +
+
[WARNING]
====
Automation pulls metadata directly into the YouTube description.
If the mapping is incorrect, *public metadata will be permanently flawed upon upload.*
====

. *Set the Review Status:*

[cols="1,3", options="header"]
|===
|Status |Meaning

|*Done*
|Content is verified and ready for upload. The entry is color-coded *Green*, signaling
it is ready for the automation pipeline.

|*Unusable*
|Select when: the audio is completely corrupted; slide capture is buggy and unreadable;
Admin has not indicated the footage is salvageable; or watching it provides no value
due to errors. The entry appears *Yellow* and will not be uploaded. +
_If anything is salvageable, mark it Incomplete instead._
|===
