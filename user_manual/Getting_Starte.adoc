=== How to Access the Cutter

. Navigate to the IP address provided by your Team Lead.
. Enter your name as the *Display Name*. +
  _This ensures all work and notes are properly attributed to you for audit and troubleshooting._
. Enter the *shared password* for your role, then click *Login*. +
  _Your shared password is provided in the volunteer official communication channel._

=== Status List

After logging in, you will see the *Status List* — items named by day and room, matching
the SCALE Linux Room YouTube channel naming.

[cols="1,3", options="header"]
|===
|Status |Meaning

|*(NO VID)*
|No talks on schedule — no action needed. OR Admin needs to map video.
Annotations often clarify the difference.

|*(NEEDS CUT)*
|Talks are uploaded and need to be cut.

|*(NEEDS REVIEW)*
|Talks are uploaded and need review.

|*(DONE)*
|All talks have been edited and reviewed — no action needed.
|===

=== Talk Status Colors

After selecting a room from the Status List, individual talks are color-coded by status:

[cols="1,3", options="header"]
|===
|Color |Meaning

|Yellow
|Marked *Unusable* by an Editor and Reviewer.

|Green
|Marked *Done* by an Editor and Reviewer.

|Yellow-Grey
|Marked *Unusable* by an Editor; currently in Reviewing status.

|Green-Grey
|Marked *Done* by an Editor; currently in Reviewing status.
|===
